Calibration Instructions

What is calibration?

We need to calibrate the eye tracker to understand where you're looking on the screen.

How it works:

  1. Read the LEFT paragraph - A text will appear on the left side of the screen. Read it naturally, moving your eyes across the words.
  2. Read the RIGHT paragraph - After 15 seconds, a text will appear on the right side. Read it the same way.
  3. Stay still - Keep your head in the same position throughout calibration.

Important:

  • This takes about 30 seconds total
  • Read naturally - don't force your eyes to specific spots
  • If calibration fails, you can restart it anytime
  • Stay in the same position for the entire experiment
